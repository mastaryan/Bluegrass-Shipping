Rates

Rates may vary based on expedited delivery options and distance. We offer several different rates to meet your needs.

Bronze Courier Service Rate

Our Bronze Courier Service offers same day delivery in the Pulaski, Laurel, Whitley, Knox, Jackson and Rockcastle area. The $1.00 per mile with a $15 minimum. Delivery is guaranteed by the end of the day.

Silver Courier Service Rate

Our Silver Courier Service offers same day, in state, rush delivery within a few hours of order placement. $1.25 per mile with a $20 minimum.

Gold Courier Service Rate

Our Gold Courier Service is a direct-run to arrive as quickly as possible is $1.50 per mile with a $25 minimum.

Out of State Courier Service Rate

Out of State Courier Service will depend on several factors such as distance and expected time for delivery. Contact us for a custom quote.

Surcharges

After Hours: Shipping request from the hours of 7:00 p.m. and 7:00 a.m. Monday through Thursday and from 7:00 p.m. Friday through 7:00 a.m. Monday are subject to a $25 additional charge.
Waiting Time: Our drivers will wait up to 15 minutes at pick-up or delivery. After 15 minutes, you are subject to a $0.25 per minute wait fee.
Weight: Any single pallet load is subject to additional weight fees. Please contact us for a custom quote.
Fuel: While we base our prices off the average fuel prices in Kentucky, this is monitored weekly and will be adjusted as necessary. Customers will know up front if there is any fuel surcharge.
